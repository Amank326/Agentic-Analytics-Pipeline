Data Integration Specifications

1

Connect Data Source

Open Power BI Desktop. Select **Get Data** -> **Text/CSV** and select the generated cleaned_store_sales.csv, or choose **MySQL database** and connect to your schema.

2

Configure Key Measures (DAX)

Click **New Measure** and write these analytics expressions:

Total Sales = SUM(store_sales[sales])
Total Profit = SUM(store_sales[profit])
Profit Margin = DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Sales], 0)
Total Quantity = SUM(store_sales[quantity])
3

Establish Dashboard Visuals

Drag and drop the following visualizations onto a 16:9 canvas:

  • KPI Cards: Display Total Sales, Total Profit, and Profit Margin.
  • Double-Axis Combo Chart: Sales and Profit by Category and Sub-category.
  • Interactive Scatter Plot: Sales (X) vs. Profit (Y) grouped by Customer.
